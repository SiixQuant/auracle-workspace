# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Quantitative traders and fund operators working inside the Auracle IDE
(an Electron agent-workspace). They are mid-task — researching, drafting,
deploying, and monitoring trading strategies against their own
self-hosted Auracle engine. Money is involved; trust is earned by
precision and honesty, not decoration.

## Product Purpose

The auracle-pack extension surfaces (Research, Live, Monitors, QC Import,
Flow — opened from custom gutter buttons) are the product's finance
features inside the IDE. They exist to move a user from "fresh research"
to "running strategy" with the fewest possible controls. Success: a
panel is glanceable in two seconds, actionable in one click, and never
lies about what the engine knows.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, institutional. The tool disappears into the task; the
data carries the color. Confidence comes from density done well and
states that always tell the truth.

## Anti-references

- The marketing site's display-serif/mystique voice — wrong register here.
- Dashboard-SaaS decoration: gradient accents, hero metrics, icon-grid
  card walls, glassmorphism.
- Vibe-y AI-tool chrome (glow borders, orbs, sparkle icons).

## Design Principles

1. **Native first.** Pack panels must be indistinguishable from the
   IDE's own surfaces — same tokens, same control vocabulary. An
   extension that looks bolted-on is a defect.
2. **One primary action per panel.** Everything else is quiet or behind
   a disclosure. Configuration earns its place or waits.
3. **States are the design.** Loading skeletons shaped like content,
   empty states that teach, errors that name the cause and the fix —
   including "engine outdated" as distinct from "engine unreachable".
4. **Engine-computed honesty.** Numbers arrive ranked and labeled with
   their origin; the client never re-scores, never invents, never hides
   a failure behind a spinner.
5. **Density with rhythm.** Compact rows, tabular numerals, hairline
   borders; whitespace spent where it groups, not where it pads.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keyboard reachable and focus-visible on every control; WCAG AA contrast
on text (the IDE is dark-theme-first); reduced-motion honored on all
animation (skeleton shimmer degrades to static, transitions to instant).
